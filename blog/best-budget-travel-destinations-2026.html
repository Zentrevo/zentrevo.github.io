💡 5 Smart Tips to Stretch Your Travel Budget in 2026

1. Fly Mid-Week

Tuesday/Wednesday flights are often 20–40% cheaper than weekend departures.

2. Use Local Transport

Skip taxis — metros, buses, and bikes save big and give authentic experiences.

3. Eat Where Locals Eat

Avoid tourist-trap restaurants; seek out markets & street stalls for real flavors.

4. Travel Light

Carry-on only = no baggage fees + more flexibility to move around.

5. Book Experiences Locally

Often cheaper and more authentic than pre-booking online. Use Klook for best deals!
